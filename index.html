How we start this?

"We just decided to."

With a Don-Quixote-style professional idealism, the ACN News Department Charlie Skinner decided to create a new show with Will McAvoy -a popular anchor and a moderate Republican who rarely shared his true political view, Will's former colleague MacKenzie McHale -a talented reporter who covered stories from Afghanistan, Pakistan and Iraq before coming back to US, and the team at ACN News.
This new show named "News Night with Will McAvoy" focuses not on shallow, short-term human interest stories but on delivering coverage of important issues and relevant matters that the American public should be fully informed on, especially in the voting booth.

"Hear me now, O thou bleak and unbearable world. Thou art base and debauched as can be. But a knight with his banners all bravely unfurled now hurls down his gauntlet to thee!"
