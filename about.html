Atlantis Cable News (ACN) is an American television network, owned by Atlantis World Media (AWM). Ms. Leona Lansing is the CEO of AWM and her son Reese Lansing is the president of AWM. ACN's headquarter is located in Manhattan, New York. It also has divisions in Washington D.C., Los Angeles, and other big cities. ACN is always trying to create an environment where news producers can report news from a fair angle without compromising with politics or ratings.
